  • Send transactional notifications — confirmations, status updates, invoices — via email and SMS

5. User Rights & Choices

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDP Act), customers have the following rights:

  • Right to access: Request a summary of personal data Referent advisors holds about you
  • Right to correction: Request correction of inaccurate or outdated information
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of data (subject to statutory retention obligations)
  • Right to grievance redressal: Raise a complaint with our Grievance Officer
  • Right to nominate: Nominate another person to exercise these rights on your behalf
  • Opt-out: Customers may opt out of non-transactional emails via the unsubscribe link

To exercise any right, email referentadvisorss@gmail.com with subject "Data Rights Request". We will respond within 30 days.

6. Data Security Measures

  • All data in transit is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • Database access is protected by Row Level Security (RLS) — each customer's data is isolated
  • Payment card and bank data is handled by our PCI-DSS compliant payment processor; we store only transaction reference IDs
  • Admin access is restricted solely to authorised Referent advisors personnel
  • Documents related to order fulfillment are stored in secure, access-controlled cloud storage

In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you within 72 hours as required under the DPDP Act, 2023.
